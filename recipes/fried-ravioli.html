Home Made Fried Ravioli

There are always so many options ranging from super indulgent desserts to your favorite cheesy, full of flavor, bite-sized appetizers. We could easily make an entire meal out of our favorite appetizers, and fried ravioli is at the top of that list of favorites.

Ingredients

  • 24 Ounces ravioli
  • 2 cup Italian-style bread crumbs
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 teaspoon black peper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

Preparation steps

  1. In a deep skillet, pour in oil and heat over medium high heat to 350 degrees F
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix together bread crumbs, flour, salt and pepper.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and eggs until well combined
  4. Working in small batches, take a ravioli, dip in egg/milk mixture and then into the flour/bread crumb mixture. Place in hot oil.
  5. Fry the raviolis for about 3-5 minutes or until golden brown on the exterior, flip as needed.
  6. Sprinkle while hot with shredded parmesan cheese and parsley. Serve Hot!
